About The Artist

Adil Bayramov

Adil Bayramov is a renowned Azerbaijani musician and a master of the TAR, the national instrument of Azerbaijan. With a career spanning over 40 years, he has established himself as one of the most distinguished performers of traditional Azerbaijani music.

Honored with the prestigious title "People's Artist of Azerbaijan Republic," Adil Bayramov has dedicated his life to preserving and promoting the rich musical heritage of Azerbaijan through his exceptional TAR performances.

Throughout his illustrious career, he has performed at numerous national and international venues, enchanting audiences with his profound understanding of mugham and other traditional Azerbaijani musical forms. His technical mastery and emotional depth have earned him recognition as one of the most influential TAR players of his generation.

As an educator, he has mentored countless young musicians, passing down his knowledge and passion for the TAR to future generations, ensuring the continuation of Azerbaijan's musical traditions.
